## Reporting a Vulnerability

**DO NOT OPEN AN ISSUE** for security exploits.

If you discover a vulnerability in ThemisAevra Core (e.g. buffer overflow in the safety controller), please email `security@themisaevra.com`.

We will acknowledge your report within 24 hours. We offer a bug bounty program for critical safety exploits.
